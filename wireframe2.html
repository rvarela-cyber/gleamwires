The Company Behind the Work

Gleam is a commercial and residential exterior cleaning company based in Dallas-Fort Worth, serving clients across all 50 states.

We handle pressure washing, window cleaning, and parking lot striping for everyone from homeowners to Fortune 10 retailers. One location or five hundred‚Äîsame team, same quality, same communication.

Gleam was built on a simple idea: exterior cleaning should come with reliability, communication, and accountability. We have the experience, the tools and the team to make that happen.

Why We're Different

You Talk to the People Who Run the Company

When you call Gleam, you're not going through a call center or a $15/hour operator reading a script. You get direct access to the principals who make decisions and solve problems on the spot. No work orders. No ticketing systems. No "someone will get back to you."

You call. We answer. It's that simple.

We Document Everything

Before photos. After photos. Sign-off from your location. A complete package delivered when the job is done. This isn't just for your peace of mind. It's for your records. When corporate asks if the work got done, you have proof. When the next inspection comes around, you have documentation.

No guessing. No chasing. Everything documented.

We Actually Clean‚ÄîNot Just Rinse

There's a difference between spraying water on a surface and actually cleaning it. We use heated water because cold water doesn't cut through grease. We pop the gum instead of spraying over it. We move the benches and clean the corners instead of working around them.

When our work dries, it still looks clean.

National Reach, Local Accountability

We service all 50 states through a vetted subcontractor network we've built over the past decade. But you still deal with us. Not a random contractor we found last week. Every subcontractor has been vetted for equipment, professionalism, and quality. They get paid fast, so they prioritize our work.

One point of contact. Consistent quality regardless of location.

We Pay Our Vendors Fast‚ÄîAnd That Benefits You

We pay our team within 24 hours of job completion. Why does that matter to you? Because vendors who get paid fast show up fast. They prioritize our jobs. They don't cut corners to move on to the next client who might actually pay them this month.

Our payment terms show up in the quality of the work.

We Don't Close the Job Until You're Satisfied

We don't finish a job and disappear. If something's not right, we come back. The next day. The same day if needed. As many times as it takes. Other vendors close the ticket and move on. We stay until the work meets the standard you were promised.

Your satisfaction isn't a policy. It's how we stay in business.

Our Core Values

Show Up

Do what you said you were going to do. Be where you said you were going to be. It sounds basic because it is. Most problems start when someone doesn't show up.

Communicate

If something changes, say so. If there's a problem, flag it. If the job is done, confirm it. Silence creates uncertainty. We don't leave clients wondering.

Do the Work Right

Not the fastest version. Not the cheapest version. The right version. Corners cleaned. Gum removed. Documentation complete. The job isn't done until it's done correctly.

Take Ownership

When something goes wrong‚Äîand sometimes it does‚Äîwe own it. No excuses. No finger-pointing. We fix it and make sure it doesn't happen again.

Treat Vendors Like Partners

Our subcontractors are the face of our company at job sites across the country. We pay them fast. We treat them with respect. We hold them to high standards because we've built a relationship worth protecting.

Earn It Every Time

Past work doesn't guarantee future business. Every job is a chance to prove we're worth keeping around. We don't take clients for granted.

Not Sure What You Need?

Tell us about your property and we'll recommend the right services. Or just claim your free power wash and see what we can do.
