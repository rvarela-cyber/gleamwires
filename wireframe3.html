Compactors Restaurant Group ‚Äî 22 Locations, EPA Compliance

Zero Compactor Citations Since Day One

The Client

A multi-location restaurant group with 22 locations across Texas and California. High waste volume. Grease, food waste, and organic matter creating compliance concerns. California locations under particular scrutiny from regulators.

The Challenge

Health inspectors were citing locations for compactor conditions. Grease buildup. Odor issues. Potential EPA violations. California locations were at risk for significant fines.

Some compactors hadn't been properly cleaned in years. One location had larvae. Another had contamination that bordered on biohazard.

The Solution

We implemented quarterly compactor cleaning across all 22 locations. Full disassembly, decontamination, and compliance documentation. Built a preventative maintenance program designed around health inspection schedules.

Every cleaning included EPA-ready documentation showing the work was done, when it was done, and what condition the equipment was in.
